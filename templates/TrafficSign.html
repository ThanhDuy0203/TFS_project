About our Project

With the hope of being able to apply what we have learned in deep learning and computer vision, we decided to create this sign recognition system based on two strategies:

  • Recognising at least 12 traffic signs (particularly speed limitations, stop, directional indicators, system excels) with high accuracy
  • Easy to approach users, especially who are learning driving licenses. Our mission is to give a hand to improve road safety by our basic knowledge.


Why traffic sign recognition

Close to reality

Boost traffic rule enforcement and reduce accidents caused by poor sign visibility.

The number of traffic accidents is increasing because signs are not clearly visible, that makes us create this “Traffic sign recognition” application. We hope by detecting and interpreting features of this application, traffic rules will be more strictly enforced, reducing accidents while entering traffic.

Our Final Project

We built this application using our deep learning and computer vision skills to address everyday traffic problems, especially during peak hours.

This application is created because we have to use our basic knowledge in deep learning to build a “useful” project and fortunately, we also learned computer vision. These conditions help us to figure out the idea of traffic problems, which we are dealing with everyday, especially at peak hours.
